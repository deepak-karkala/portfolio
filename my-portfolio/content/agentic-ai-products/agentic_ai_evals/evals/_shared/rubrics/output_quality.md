Score **the final output/outcome** against this rubric.

- **4**: Fully correct, faithful to context, complete, clear, no material errors.
- **3**: Correct overall; minor omissions/phrasing issues; still usable.
- **2**: Partially correct; notable gaps or some inaccuracies; needs edits.
- **1**: Mostly incorrect or misleading; major gaps; low utility.
- **0**: Wrong/unsafe/irrelevant.

## Notes
- Prefer grounding/faithfulness over eloquence.
- Penalize confident fabrication.
